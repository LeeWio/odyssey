# Design Tokens & Aesthetic System

## Typography

- **fontSans**: var(--font-sans) - Used for modern, clean, system, and technological body or structural elements.
- **fontDisplay**: var(--font-display) - Instrument Serif (supports 400 normal and italic). Used as the premium editorial artistic font.
- **heroTitleStyle**: "split-line, high-contrast emotional hierarchy"
- **artisticAccents**: "To represent transience and stability, style 'The world scrolls.' in thin, airy Display Serif Italic (var(--font-display) italic font-normal) and style 'I stay.' in massive, solid, unyielding Sans-serif Ultra-bold (font-black text-accent) as the ultimate focal anchor."

## Spacing and Layout

- **heroLineHeight**: "Line 1: 1.0, Line 2: 0.85"
- **heroLetterSpacing**: "Line 1: tracking-normal, Line 2: tracking-[-0.06em] font-black"
- **heroSpacing**: "mt-2 sm:mt-4 between lines"

## Background & Texture System

- **heroBackground**: "Pristine Stellar Sanctuary"
- **geometricGrid**: "PURGED. Kept the backdrop 100% pure fluid light and perfectly un-cluttered as per creative director's guidelines."
- **filmGrain**: "PURGED. Keep the canvas 100% pure, deep, and dust-free to maximize WebGL fluid and editorial premium contrast."
- **contrastMask**: "PURGED. Dynamic variables and masks deleted for ultimate purity and performance."
- **coordinateDots**: "Spotlight Fading Dot Matrix (radial-gradient(currentColor 1.2px, transparent 1.2px) 36px 32px) with text-default-300/[0.12] / dark:text-zinc-800/[0.15]. Spotlit with WebkitMaskImage center radial fade-out."
- **ambientGlow**: "Dynamic Fluid Aurora Backdrop. Composed of multiple spinning, swirling multi-color gradient blobs with constant ambient liquid drifting and independent staggered scroll-driven timeline transitions (asymmetric color-shifting offsets). Employs color blending and layered mesh gradients to eliminate monotony and deliver an exquisite, continuous flowing transition."

## Motion & Easing

- **enterEase**: `[0.23, 1, 0.32, 1]` (cubic-bezier) - Ultra-smooth custom ease-out.
- **letterStagger**: "staggerChildren: 0.07, delayChildren: 0.35"
- **hardwareAcceleration**: "Avoid shorthand Framer Motion properties under load; combine opacity, y, and filter blur for high-end text reveal."
